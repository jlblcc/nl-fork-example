Collaborative Conservation for a Changing North

The vast and largely intact landscapes of Alaska and Northwest Canada support unique natural and cultural resources that are valued locally and globally. As climate-related impacts and land use changes rapidly alter these social-ecological systems, a new approach to conservation is needed. Landscape Conservation Cooperatives (LCCs) are an international collaborative effort that identifies shared conservation goals and a means to achieve them. Northern Latitudes is the leadership of the five LCCs within Alaska and Northern Canada and LCC and the Alaska Climate Science Center staff.
